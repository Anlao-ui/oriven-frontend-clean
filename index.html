Questions

Frequently asked.

What is ORIVEN?
ORIVEN is a Brand Intelligence Platform. Unlike standard AI tools that generate from blank context, ORIVEN learns your complete brand identity first — then generates every asset from that foundation. The result is consistent, on-brand content without constant re-prompting.
What is BrandCore?
BrandCore is the central identity system inside ORIVEN. It stores everything that defines your brand — name, positioning, colors, fonts, tone of voice, target audience, and competitive differentiation. Every generation ORIVEN produces is filtered through your BrandCore automatically.
Can I generate UGC videos?
Yes. ORIVEN includes a full UGC Creator tool powered by HeyGen. Choose from real AI creators, select a voice, and let ORIVEN’s AI write a brand-aligned script. Videos are generated in minutes and can be used directly as ad creatives.
Who is ORIVEN for?
Founders, startups, e-commerce brands, and marketing teams who need to create high-quality brand assets quickly without sacrificing consistency. Especially powerful for small teams where one person handles brand and marketing.
Do I need design experience?
No. ORIVEN is designed for non-designers. If you’re starting from scratch, the AI generates your entire brand identity from a single sentence description.
