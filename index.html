About Oriven

Built to solve a real problem
in advertising.

Marketing data is scattered across platforms. Oriven brings it together so you can spend less time in dashboards and more time making better decisions.

Our Story

Oriven started from
a real frustration.

While working on e-commerce projects and running advertising campaigns, I constantly found myself switching between platforms just to understand what was happening.

Google Ads, Meta Ads and TikTok Ads all provided valuable information, but none of them showed the complete picture. Important data was spread across multiple dashboards, reports and interfaces.

The more campaigns I worked on, the more obvious the problem became. Marketing data is fragmented, and that fragmentation costs businesses both time and money.

I started building Oriven to solve that problem. The goal was simple: bring marketing intelligence together in one place and help businesses make faster, better advertising decisions.

Our Mission

Marketing data is fragmented.
We’re here to fix that.

Businesses running ads across multiple platforms lose hours every week switching between tools, exporting reports, and trying to piece together a complete picture. That time and focus should go toward making better decisions — not manually stitching data together.
