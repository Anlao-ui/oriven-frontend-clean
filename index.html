Questions

Frequently asked.

What is ORIVEN?
ORIVEN is a Brand Intelligence Platform. Unlike standard AI tools that generate from blank context, ORIVEN learns your complete brand identity first — then generates every asset from that foundation. The result is consistent, on-brand content without constant re-prompting.
What is BrandCore?
BrandCore is the central identity system inside ORIVEN. It stores everything that defines your brand — your name, positioning, colors, fonts, tone of voice, target audience, key messages, and competitive differentiation. Every generation ORIVEN produces is filtered through your BrandCore automatically.
Can I generate UGC videos with ORIVEN?
Yes. ORIVEN includes a full UGC Creator tool powered by HeyGen. Choose from real AI creators, select a voice (with audio preview), and either write your own script or let ORIVEN's AI write one based on your brand. Videos are generated in minutes and can be used directly as ad creatives.
Who is ORIVEN for?
ORIVEN is built for founders, startups, e-commerce brands, and marketing teams who need to create high-quality brand assets quickly without sacrificing consistency. It's especially powerful for small teams where one person handles brand and marketing.
Do I need design or branding experience?
No. ORIVEN is designed for non-designers. If you have a brand (even a rough one), ORIVEN can formalize it into a complete BrandCore. If you're starting from scratch, the AI can generate your entire brand identity from a single sentence description.
